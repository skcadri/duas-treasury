A Treasury of Duas

from the Quran & Sunnah
General, non-situational supplications for daily recitation and reflection

This collection gathers supplications from the Quran and the authenticated Sunnah that are general in nature — not tied to a specific time, place, or circumstance. These are duas you can make at any time, in any state, as a foundation for your daily relationship with Allah.
